Rules of the game

Background

"Commander, I'm so glad you have arrived! Those pesky aliens are back again. That wasteland you see in front of you used to be a thriving city full of people. Now its just a desert. The world leaders have been in communication with the aliens and they think they have found a solution. If you beat the aliens in mental combat, they will leave us alone, well they said that last time as well. This time however, they have offered us a longer rest between their visits if you take on the harder challenges. Good luck Commander, we are all counting on you!"
