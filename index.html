Rules of the game

Background

"Commander, I'm so glad you have arrived! Those pesky aliens are back again. That wasteland you see in front of you used to be a thriving city full of people. Now its just a desert. It's quiet down there now but at night they are a lot more active. The world leaders have been in communication with the aliens and they think they have found a solution. If you beat the aliens in mental combat, they will leave us alone, well they said that last time as well. This time however, they have offered us a longer rest between their visits if you take on the harder challenges. Good luck Commander, we are all counting on you!"

How To Play

To choose the hand that you want to play click or touch the corresponding UFO. Keyboard users can tab between the options and press the 'Enter' key to select. Be sure of which hand you want to play before making your selection. This is a battle soldier and there are no second chances. The selected UFO will move to face off against the boss and will return to their original starting location once the round has been played. You will need to win a total of 5 rounds before the boss alien does in order to win the game. You can choose a different number of rounds in the settings.

Settings

You can change the difficulty, number of rounds required to win and the colour scheme within the settings tab.

Difficulty

There are two difficulty levels, easy and hard. In easy difficulty the boss appears to be selecting its tiles at random. However, in the hard difficulty the boss is much more calculated. Those mad scientists over in the research division keep saying there is a pattern but no one has been able to figure it out yet.

Rounds Required

Adjust the slider to select more or less rounds required. The aliens won't get out of bed for a game of less than 3 rounds for a win, but they will happily play for hours upto a whopping 33 rounds required for a win.

Colour Mode

There are two colour modes available for you to choose from, light or dark. The dark mode can be easier on the eyes for those late night gaming sessions.
